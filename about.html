About Us

Rooted in education. Driven by impact. Committed to sustainability.

Our Mission

The ASOD Dental Sustainability Club is a student-led organization committed to integrating environmental consciousness into dental education. We believe that sustainable practices in healthcare begin with the next generation of practitioners.

From reducing single-use plastics in clinical settings to advocating for greener dental suppliers, our members lead the charge in making oral healthcare more eco-friendly — one practice at a time.

Our Story

Founded by a group of passionate dental students who recognized the environmental footprint of modern dentistry, ASOD Sustainability Club was born out of a desire to do things differently. We saw the mountains of disposable materials, the energy consumption, and the lack of conversation around eco-conscious practice — and we decided to start that conversation.

Today, our club organizes events, produces educational content, and builds a community of future dentists who care about both their patients and the planet.
